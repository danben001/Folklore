Labhraidh Loingseach

Deirdre of the Sorrows

This website was created to continue the tradition of telling Irish myth through spoken word with the assistance of visuals. Ancient Irish stories for many years were passed down primarily through word of mouth, with more recent documented writings of them being the source for what we understand of them today.
